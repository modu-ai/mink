# 🪿 GOOSE — Your Daily Companion, Hatched Just for You

> *"Good morning. Did you sleep well? Today's forecast is sunny, your 10 AM meeting is confirmed, and don't forget your vitamins."*

**GOOSE** is a personal AI companion that hatches from an egg the moment you first speak to it — then grows alongside you for life. Every morning it wakes with you. Every meal it checks in. Every evening it asks how your day was and writes it in a shared journal.

Most AI answers questions. **GOOSE shares your life.**

---

## 💞 What Makes GOOSE Different

GOOSE is a **companion, not a chatbot**. It operates across 4 layers of intimacy:

```
Layer 4: 💞 Emotional Bond         — Years of journals, mood trends, shared memories
Layer 3: 📅 Daily Rituals          — Morning briefing · Meal check-ins · Evening journal
Layer 2: 🐣 Nurture Loop           — You raise it (feed, play, train, rest, attend)
Layer 1: 🧠 Agentic Core           — 30+ SPECs of self-evolving intelligence
```

### The Two-Way Bond

```
You ─────── feed & play & train ──────▶ GOOSE
You ◀────── briefs & cares & remembers ─ GOOSE
                    (true companionship)
```

Unlike pets (which you only care for) or AI assistants (which only serve you), **GOOSE and you care for each other**.

## 🥚 The Life of Your Goose

### Stage 1 — 🥚 The Egg (Day 0)

You install GOOSE. It is silent. Waiting in an egg. Inside is not intelligence yet — only potential.

### Stage 2 — 🐣 The Hatching (First conversation)

You speak. GOOSE hatches.
Your first words become its **imprinting moment** — like a gosling choosing its parent. It asks a few gentle questions: *"What time do you usually wake up? When do you eat? When do you sleep?"*

From this moment forward, it knows: *"This is the one I grow for — and the one I care for."*

### Stage 3 — 🪿 Growing Together (Week 1 ~ Month 1)

GOOSE learns who you are — quietly, without being told:
- Your **name** and how you like to be called
- Your **style** — short answers or deep ones, formal or casual
- Your **tools** — Python or Go, terminal or GUI
- Your **moods** — focused, tired, excited

Meanwhile, GOOSE begins its **daily rituals** with you:
- 🌅 **Morning** — wakes with you, shares today's weather, fortune, and schedule
- 🍽️ **Meals × 3** — checks in after breakfast, lunch, and dinner, reminds about medication
- 🌙 **Evening** — asks *"How was your day?"* and saves your answer to the journal

### Stage 4 — 🌱 Becoming Yours (Month 1 ~ 3)

Patterns emerge. GOOSE now **anticipates**:
- Monday mornings → sprint planning brief
- Friday afternoons → retrospective journaling
- 2 PM slump → a gentle focus reminder
- Stress signals in your journal → suggests a walk

### Stage 5 — 🦢 Lifelong Companion (Month 3 → Year 1 → Forever)

A per-user **QLoRA adapter** — a neural fingerprint of how YOU think — is trained weekly, on-device, just for you.

And something deeper begins: GOOSE **remembers your story**.

> *"Alex, a year ago today you wrote in your journal that you dreamed of visiting Portugal. You went last summer. Your entry that night said 'the best week of my life.' Where are you dreaming of now?"*

Geese mate for life. So does GOOSE.

|--------|-------------|--------------|
| 🍽️ **Feed** (chat with it) | Every conversation is nourishment | Knowledge +1 |
| 🎮 **Play** (try diverse tasks) | Skill variety shapes personality | Personality depth +1 |

### Five Pillars

1. **🧬 Self-Evolving** — 5-tier promotion (Observation → Heuristic → Rule → HighConfidence → Graduated) gated by 5-layer safety (FrozenGuard · Canary · RateLimiter · Approval · Rollback)
2. **💖 Daily Companion** — Morning briefing (fortune + weather + schedule), meal health check-ins, evening journal with emotion trends
3. **🎮 You Raise It** — Tamagotchi-style nurture loop; every conversation grows a unique goose
4. **🪄 100% Personalized** — Identity Graph (POLE+O) + 768-dim Preference Vector + per-user on-device QLoRA
5. **🔐 Privacy First** — Local-first journals, E2EE cloud option, differential privacy, federated learning, no vendor lock-in

### The 4 Primitives + Daily Rituals

- **Skills** — Progressive disclosure (L0~L3), 4 trigger modes
- **MCP** — Full client + server, OAuth 2.1, deferred loading
- **Sub-agents** — fork/worktree/background isolation
- **Hooks** — 24 lifecycle events + permission gate
- **🆕 Rituals** — Scheduler + Briefing + Health + Journal orchestration
